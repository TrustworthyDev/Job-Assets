MediaSphere — AI-Powered Multi-Platform Content Discovery & Personalization Platform
Plavno developed a scalable media platform that unifies video, articles, and interactive content across web, mobile, and Smart TV while improving discovery, personalization, and content engagement.

### Key Metrics & Impact
MediaSphere is a large content platform for educational and lifestyle media. It combines video content, articles, and interactive formats in one ecosystem and delivers a consistent user experience across devices. The product is not a children’s platform, but it demonstrates deep expertise in building complex, content-heavy, personalized digital ecosystems.

- Up to 15–20% stronger engagement after rollout of personalized discovery flows
- 10–15% better retention in the first months after platform unification
- Improved content discoverability across web, mobile, and TV surfaces
- Deeper content consumption per session after navigation and taxonomy improvements
- Better reuse of content assets across products and channels

01. Problem
Large content businesses often grow through disconnected products, fragmented taxonomies, and inconsistent user experiences across platforms.

Common challenges include:
- Separate products for web, mobile, and TV
- Weak content discovery and navigation
- Limited personalization capabilities
- Inconsistent user state between devices
- Poor content structure for search, recommendations, and SEO

This fragmentation reduces engagement, weakens retention, and makes it harder for users to find relevant content. It also slows editorial operations, increases duplication, and reduces the value of existing content assets.

02. Challenge
The platform needed to:
- Rebuild fragmented products into one unified ecosystem
- Improve content discovery and navigation
- Introduce personalization across touchpoints
- Create a consistent experience between devices
- Support web, mobile, and Smart TV environments
- Enable scalable integrations with analytics, CRM, and external APIs

There were also delivery constraints:
- Legacy content structures were inconsistent across products
- Taxonomy quality varied and reduced content reuse potential
- User-state continuity across devices had to be redesigned
- Platform teams needed better reuse of backend logic instead of maintaining separate product silos

### From Fragmentation to Scalable Discovery
MediaSphere was developed as an API-first multi-platform content ecosystem with a unified content model, recommendation system, and shared backend services. The platform centralizes video, articles, and interactive experiences into one architecture, improving discoverability, personalization, and consistency across web, mobile, and OTT environments. By combining structured content data, hybrid recommendations, and platform-aware UX patterns, MediaSphere helps users discover more relevant content while giving the business a scalable digital foundation with lower fragmentation and better content asset reuse.

# User Flows
- Discover: A user opens the platform on web, mobile, or TV and browses personalized home sections, categories, and recommendations.
- Explore: The system helps the user navigate through content types, tags, entities, and search results to find relevant material faster.
- Personalize: Recommendations are generated using a hybrid approach that combines rules, structured metadata, and machine learning signals.
- Continue Anywhere: User progress and preferences are synchronized across devices for a consistent experience across platforms.

### Deep Dive: Project Architecture
- Frontend Layer: Next.js web experience, React Native mobile app, and Smart TV applications tailored to OTT usage patterns.
- Backend Layer: Node.js services delivering content, recommendations, user-state synchronization, and platform integrations.
- Personalization Layer: Hybrid recommendation system combining rules-based logic and ML-powered personalization.
- Data & Event Processing Layer: Structured content metadata, event processing, and user engagement tracking for discovery optimization.
- Integration Layer: Connections with CRM systems, GA4 analytics, and external APIs supporting growth and measurement.
- Infrastructure Layer: Cloud architecture across AWS and GCP to support performance, flexibility, and multi-platform scaling.
