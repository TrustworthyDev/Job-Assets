### About the Project
# The Project
LifePal is a productivity AI-powered app that aims to help users seize control of their daily schedule thanks to the integration with Apple pre-built utilities for tracking different life domains – from Calendar to Health.
The client entrusted SolveIt to build a mobile app that leverages ChatGPT and a custom algorithm to deliver a personal virtual assistant experience. The app recognizes speech, formulates queries, and manages your plans.

# The Idea
While modern technology has opened up vast new possibilities, many people struggle with procrastination, a lack of focus, and an inability to find time for what truly matters. We constantly need to toggle between different tasks without pause.
Recognizing this, the client sought to create a tool blending artificial innovations and precious human values, with the former serving the latter, not the other way around. The key is to strike the balance between tech progress and personal priorities.

## The solutions
The main point of existing productivity tools was fragmentation, which required users to toggle between multiple apps and platforms to manage their tasks, calendar, notes, and other important information.
To address this, the vision was based on merging the magic of two ecosystems: artificial intelligence and Apple apps. LifePal offers its users access to a lot of well-being insights they use on a daily basis in one place.
As the target audience of LifePal is productivity-minded individuals who are likely to own Apple devices, it was decided to choose the iOS and macOS platforms. The MVP conception helped to focus on the core features.

# Monetization Model
Together with the client we have chosen a subscription monetization model to generate steady recurring revenue and encourage long-term user retention with the app's core features.
LifePal offers a range of pricing plans, including a free plan as well as monthly, quarterly, and annual subscriptions that provide increasing levels of features and functionality.
