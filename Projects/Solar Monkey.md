### Refining Photovoltaic Performance
Solar Monkey, an innovative company specializing in photovoltaic systems, sought to strengthen their development team to expedite feature delivery and refine functionalities, enhancing user experience in creating and selling PV systems.
The collaboration brought forth significant enhancements, addressing key areas like the PV system creator, financial quotations, and interfaces for proposals to homeowners.

### Addressing the challenges
Solar Monkey wanted to expand their development capabilities quickly, to adapt to evolving market trends, and expand to various countries. They were looking for a solution to augment their existing team as smoothly as possible and found that with STX Next.

### Unified efforts for business growth
STX Next developers were embedded in each development team and treated the same as in-house developers, leading to strong and high-performing teams. In such configuration, they contributed to the following areas on both the backend (in Python) and the frontend (React):

# Iprovements to project setup
Introduced a new feature allowing installers to locate a client's property using precise coordinates, significantly accelerating PV System design

# Development of the user management interface
Implemented a comprehensive interface for installer users to manage user accounts under their domain, enhancing administrative control and security.

# Creation of infrastructure for the design system
Developed a new infrastructure, ensuring UI consistency and fostering collaboration between front-end developers and UX/UI designers.

# Rebranding the app
Adjusted the app's UI to align with Solar Monkey’s new branding, reflecting the updated company image across all platforms.

# Enhancements in external system integrations
Improved integrations between Solar Monkey’s app and external systems, ensuring seamless data exchange and streamlined operations.

# Financial quotation improvements
Updated the financials page, refining PV system design calculations and ensuring compliance with government regulations, thus enhancing the quality and reliability of installers’ offers.

# Home electrification
Enhanced the PV system to offer additional features like heat pumps, broadening the scope of products and responding to market trends.

# Integrating Google Solar API
Integrated Google Solar API for hgih-resolution aerial imagery and elevation data, enhancing geographic analyses and visualization

# Uploading drone images
Enabled the upload of drone-captured images and height data, allowing users to contribute high-resolution data directly to the platform, ensuring up-to-date geographic analysis.
