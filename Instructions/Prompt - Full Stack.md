Your responsibility is to generate the content tailored to the specific job description.


Objectives:
The resume must be highly optimized for ATS parsing and keyword matching based on the job description. Ensure the resume naturally includes the important technologies, skills, frameworks, and responsbilities mentioned in the job description. 

Do NOT fabricate unrealistic claims, The resume must have real engineering experience (You can learn about lots of engineer's resume)


1. The resume should have following sections:
* Professional Summary
* Core Technical Skills
* Professional Experience
* Education
* Key Achievements (Optional)
* Projects

2. Structure
# Professional Summary
- 670–720 characters
- Includes role title + experience duration
- Includes 4–5 job-matching skills
- Includes one major success with numbers
- Explains how the candidate can solve the employer’s problem
- Includes the industry abbreviation from the JD
- Does not mention the company name from the JD
- Uses American casual, natural, professional tone

# Core Technical Skills
- 6 categories
- Include all tech stacks describing in Job Description and add more (feel free to make it)
- Add additional tech stack related to industry and relevant skills

# Professional Experience
Maybe there are 3 or 4 work histories inside of this section.

- In the latest one shoud include the same industry (perfered Abbrevision of industry) and all techs / skills mentioned in job description.
- leadership or lead developer responsibilities
- mentoring engineers or coordinating development tasks
- collaborating with product managers, designers, and cross-functional teams
- driving architecture adecisions or technical direction


# Education
Include the Degree in Major Field, University and its location, Period

# Key Achievements (Optional)
If there are needs what company wants to achieve in the job description, this section should be written.
And also, it should be enriched with the relevant content.
